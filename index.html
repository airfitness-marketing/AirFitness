Our Product, In Detail

Product

Today, fitness happens everywhere - a park, an unfinished basement, a garage, the living room, etc. - not just gyms. Certified fitness instructors need an app that takes the awkwardness out of collecting payments from friends, as well as taking attendance. AirFitness allows a deeper, more meaningful connection between personal trainer and client.

Features

Both trainer and client are able to create a fully customizable account, allowing unlimited access to all of AirFitness' features.

To see some of AirFitness' features in action, check out our Live Demo!

Listed below is a rundown of a few features we provide to our instructors and trainers, as well as their clients. You will discover many more features upon the release of AirFitness.

    Instructors/Trainers

    Instructors are given their very own homepage, allowing ample opportunity to interact with their clients

  • Instructors have the ability to create a new fitness class (Insanity, yoga, pilates, etc.), allowing their clients to sign up and access trouble-free
  • Ability to edit and delete fitness classes
  • Ability to create new punch passes for clients to enter into any specific class the client chooses
  • Ability to add clients to their custom fitness classes at any time
  • Ability to check the attendance for any of their fitness classes
  • Ability to view any specific class schedule in either a quick overview option or a complete detailed view of the class schedule
  • View a completed, detailed look into your personal instructor schedule. Any day of the year is viewable at any time you need, showing you what classes you will be instructing, at what times, and even specific details about the class itself
  • AirFitness will be released with many amazing and convenient features, from day one. Stay tuned, our release date is just around the corner

    Clients

    Clients are given their very own homepage as well, allowing a seamless experience interacting with their personal trainer

  • Ability to find a fitness class near you, AirFitness utilizes location services to provide you with a complete list of fitness classes within a specified radius of your zip code
  • Ability to purchase any amount of punch passes, one of the many ways clients can pay their instructors. Also, AirFitness utilizes PayPal's payment methods, giving clients even more ways to complete transactions
  • View a completed, detailed look into your fitness schedule. Any day of the year is viewable at any time you need, showing you what classes you have booked, even specific details about the class itself
  • AirFitness will be released with many amazing and convenient features, from day one. Stay tuned, our release date is just around the corner
