Our Product, In Detail

Product

Today, fitness happens everywhere - a park, an unfinished basement, a garage, the living room, etc. - not just gyms. Certified fitness instructors need an app that takes the awkwardness out of collecting payments from friends and taking attendance. AirFitness allows a deeper, more meaningful connection between personal trainer and client.

Features

Both trainer and client are able to create a fully customizable account, allowing unlimited access to all of AirFitness' features. To see AirFitness' features in action, check out our Live Demo!
