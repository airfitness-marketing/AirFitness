About Cobrettie

Hello, my name is Cobrettie Garner. I am a Full Stack Web Developer, currently located in Central Florida. Like some of you, I discovered the field of software engineering much later on in my life, after years of chasing many different ideas and dreams. Don't let me fool you, I'm still chasing, however, chasing something a bit different. We learn as time goes on, or at least we hope to learn, right? Long before my first line of code was ever written, I was a successful business owner. The business was exceeding every expectation I could have ever had. As time passed me by, the wake-up call hit me. I want more out of my life. Yes, business was great, but it simply was not what I wanted for the rest of my life, and so began the journey of becoming a Software Engineer. I am currently seven months into this journey, and I continue to love every single second of it. One of my ultimate goals in life is to create products that will better the lives of those less fortunate.
